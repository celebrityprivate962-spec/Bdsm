Our Core Values

These principles guide every aspect of our community and ensure a safe, respectful environment for all members.

Consent

All activities require clear, enthusiastic, and ongoing consent. We promote understanding of consent as an active, continuous process.

🛡️

Safety

We prioritize physical, emotional, and psychological safety through education, awareness, and established protocols.

🤝

Respect

We honor individual choices, boundaries, and identities. Everyone deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.

🔐

Privacy

We maintain strict confidentiality and provide secure spaces where members can explore safely without judgment.
