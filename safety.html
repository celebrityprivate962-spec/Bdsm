Safety & Community Guidelines

Building trust through awareness, communication, and respect

Safety is the foundation of our community. We believe that responsibility, awareness, and mutual respect are essential for creating an environment where everyone can explore, learn, and connect safely. By prioritizing emotional and physical well-being, we ensure that our community remains a trusted space for all members.

Core Safety Principles

These fundamental values guide every interaction within our community

Consent Communication Respect Responsibility
🔒

✓

✓
Accept boundaries without question
✓

🛡️

Responsibility

Understanding risks and acting with care. Taking ownership of your actions and their potential impact on others.

✓
Assess risks honestly
✓
Have safety measures in place
✓
Be accountable for your actions

Understanding Personal Limits

Personal boundaries are essential for a safe and enjoyable experience. Everyone has different comfort levels, triggers, and preferences. It's crucial to understand that:

  • Boundaries can change over time or in different contexts
  • People may have different comfort levels even in similar situations
  • It's important to respect "no" immediately and without question
  • Negotiating boundaries is an ongoing process, not a one-time conversation

Remember that setting and respecting boundaries is an act of care for yourself and others.

Safe Practices

General guidelines for responsible engagement

📋

Plan Ahead

Discuss intentions, boundaries, and expectations before engaging in any activity. Planning helps ensure everyone is on the same page.

⚠️

Stay Aware

Maintain awareness of physical and emotional limits during interactions. Check in regularly and be ready to pause or stop if needed.

🚫

Avoid Pressure

Never pressure or coerce someone into doing something they're uncomfortable with. Enthusiasm cannot exist under pressure.

❤️

Prioritize Well-being

Always put physical and emotional well-being first. If something doesn't feel right, take a step back and reassess.

Community Rules

Standards that ensure a safe and respectful environment

1

No Harassment or Discrimination

2

Never share private information about other members without explicit consent.

4

5

Report Concerns

Reporting & Support

If you encounter any behavior that violates our guidelines or makes you feel unsafe, please report it immediately. Your concerns will be taken seriously and handled confidentially.

Building a Positive Community Together

Trust, safety, and mutual respect form the foundation of our community. By following these guidelines, we create an environment where everyone can explore, learn, and connect authentically. Remember that your actions contribute to the overall atmosphere of our community.

Thank you for helping us maintain a space that values the well-being of all members.
